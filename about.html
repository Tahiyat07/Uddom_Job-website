About Us


At Uddom, we provide teenagers with real job opportunities to gain practical skills,
even without prior experience.Our platform offers flexible, hour-based jobs that
help teens work around their schedules while learning from real-life experiences.
We also connect people who need temporary help with teenagers ready
for part-time work.By checking their profiles, anyone can hire help for a
short time, while teens gain valuable experience. Whether you’re a teen
seeking experience or someone needing assistance, we’re here to make it easy for you.
